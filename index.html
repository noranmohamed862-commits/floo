📅 Booking an Appointment
1
Describe Symptoms AI
Patient types what they feel — AI suggests the right medical specialty automatically.
2
Choose a Doctor
List of available doctors in that specialty — with ratings, wait time, and past visits flagged.
3
Pick a Date & Time
Calendar view showing available slots in real-time. Busy times are grayed out.
4
Pay Online
Card · wallet · insurance — transparent fee shown before confirming.
5
Confirmed Auto Notify
Instant notification sent. Reminder 24 hours before the appointment.
📁 Medical File & History
🔍
Past DiagnosesAll previous conditions on a scrollable timeline
💊
PrescriptionsCurrent and past medications with dosage
🧪
Lab ResultsAll tests with trend graphs (glucose, BP…)
📡
ImagingX-rays and scans viewable inline
💬 Chat with Doctor
1
Open Chat with Assigned Doctor
Patient finds their doctor's card and opens the chat window.
2
Send Message, Photo, or File
Can attach an image of a report, skin condition, or any related document.
3
Doctor Replies Notification
Patient notified when doctor responds. Full conversation history saved.
🤖 AI Health Assistant AI
1
Patient types how they feel
"I have had a headache for 3 days with fever."
2
AI asks follow-up questions
To understand severity — pain level, location, duration, other symptoms.
3
AI gives a recommendation
Suggests a specialty, urgency level, and nearest available appointment slot.
🚨 Emergency
1
Tap the Emergency Button
A red high-visibility button always visible on the home screen.
2
Select Main Symptom
Large tap chips: Chest pain · Can't breathe · Unconscious · Severe bleeding.
3
Alert Sent Immediately URGENT
Location + symptoms + full medical history sent to the emergency team at once.

Doctor Flow
Cases, examinations, prescriptions, and follow-ups
🏠 Doctor Dashboard
📋
Today's QueueFull patient list with appointment times
🚨
Urgent CasesHigh-risk patients pinned at the top
🔔
NotificationsLab ready · follow-up due · new message
💬
Patient MessagesUnread messages from all patients
🏢 Department & Schedule
1
Doctor selects their Department
On first setup — picks specialty and department. Can be in more than one.
2
Sets Available Hours
Marks working days and available time slots — patients can only book during these times.
3
Dashboard shows today's load
Number of appointments, cases done vs. remaining, and any urgent flags.
📂 Opening a Patient Case
1
Select Patient from Queue
Tap on the patient's name to open their full case view.
2
See AI Case Summary AI
Auto-generated summary: chief complaint, history highlights, and risk level shown instantly.
3
Review Medical History & Labs
Past diagnoses, current medications, and latest lab results — all on one screen. Abnormal values highlighted automatically.
4
Add Notes & Close Visit
Doctor writes visit notes, marks diagnosis, and the case is saved to the patient's timeline.
💊 Writing a Prescription
1
Doctor types the diagnosis
Free text or select from a list of common conditions.
2
AI suggests treatment AI
Recommends medication, dosage adjusted for this patient's age and weight, with safety check included.
3
Confirm & Send Auto Notify
Prescription sent to patient instantly. Patient receives a notification with full medication details.
🧪 Lab Requests & Results
1
Doctor requests a test
Selects from the lab menu — blood work, imaging, culture, etc. — and assigns to the patient.
2
Result arrives Notification
Doctor notified when results are uploaded. Abnormal values are flagged in red automatically.
3
Review & act
Doctor reviews and can update the prescription or schedule a follow-up directly from the results screen.
